# Groundwater Trends in Gujarat

## Long-term trend
Gujarat has shown an overall groundwater decline of 2–4 meters in many over-exploited blocks over the past 10–15 years.

## Annual decline rate
Average annual decline in critical and over-exploited blocks:
- 0.2 to 0.5 meters per year (varies by district)

## Highly affected districts
- Banaskantha
- Patan
- Mehsana
- Kachchh
