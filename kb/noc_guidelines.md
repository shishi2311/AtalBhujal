# NOC for Groundwater Extraction (General Guidance)

## Who needs an NOC?
Industries, infrastructure, and mining projects typically require NOC for extraction from CGWA/State Authority.

## Basic conditions (illustrative)
- Water-efficient technologies must be used.
- Rainwater harvesting & recharge structures are recommended/mandatory per category.
- Metering and regular reporting may be required.

## How to apply (high-level)
- Identify jurisdiction (CGWA or State).
- Prepare required documents (site details, extraction qty, maps).
- Submit application via the official portal; track status online.

> Always refer to the latest official notifications/portal for state-specific conditions.
